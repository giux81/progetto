Human and car, marriage or funeral?
The number of car accidents per year is a relevant parameter for a nation, indeed this kind of events can result in fatalities and injuries. Therefore, it is important to monitor and prevent road accidents. In the following sections, we present data related to this issue in Italy, spanning from 1991 to 2022. Our objective is to inform the government about the current situation so that it can take the necessary actions to reduce the number of road accidents.
Incidents' map
Whole numbers
In the graph, we see that, in absolute numbers, Rome and Milan have the highest number of accidents. This is expected due to their high population density. However, this representation is essential as it helps authorities understand where the greatest need for assistance vehicles is, facilitating better management of accident consequences.

On the other hand, this chart shows the number of accidents per capita. This information is vital because it helps us identify areas where enhancing road safety is needed, emphasizing the importance of prevention and citizen education.
